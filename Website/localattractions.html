Hart County, Kentucky has a deep connection to history and nature. From being just a simple drive away from Mammoth Cave to a 30-minute drive to see Abraham Lincolnâ€™s birthplace there is just so much to do in a short distance and we have compiled a list of some of these amazing places you may want to visit.



Mammoth Cave National Park

The mammoth Cave National Park offers a variety of family friendly activities. From Hiking to enjoying the history of the longest cave system to date there is a little something for all nature lovers.



Dutch Country Safari Park

The Dutch Country Safari Park offers a fun time for people of all ages. The safari tour can be done by car or by hay ride, which gives everyone the chance to pet some truly unique and exotic animals like a zebra and water buffalo. There is also a petting zoo, a train ride for the younger kids, an inside reptile and bird exhibit, as well as a gift shop.



Abraham Lincoln's Birthplace National Park

Abraham Lincoln's Birthplace is home to the original Lincoln's Memmorial and has quite the view. There is also cabin's to rent and a campground for overnight trips. A great place to learn the history of one of the most iconic presidents.
